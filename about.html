About Us

Our Vision

“To impart value-based quality education in technology and management
domain in innovative way empowering students to serve the global
society at large.”

Our Mission

To provide state-of-art infrastructure, amenities, dedicated staff,
recreation, and sports, industry-based syllabus to nurture the academic needs.
– To organize and conduct value-based technical, management, and
spiritual workshops/ programs to meet the upcoming challenges of the industry and society.
– To arrange and conduct personality development programs for
students to enhance their communication and presentation skills to develop their leadership Qualities.
– To develop our institute as a global IT hub where the world looks for
solutions to their problem/needs through our research
